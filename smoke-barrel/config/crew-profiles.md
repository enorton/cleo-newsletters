## ⚠️ IMPORTANT: Discovery Over Repetition

**The crew already owns many staples from their favorite distilleries.**

**Newsletter Strategy:**
- **DON'T** feature standard releases from Lagavulin, Ardbeg, Talisker, etc. unless:
  - It's a **new limited edition** from those distilleries
  - There's **breaking news** about them (price changes, availability issues, special releases)
  - A **rare expression** becomes available
- **DO** focus on:
  - **New discoveries** - bottles they haven't tried
  - **Emerging distilleries** worth exploring
  - **Under-the-radar finds** at LCBO or BSW
  - **Value bottles** they might have overlooked
  - **Limited releases** from any distillery
  - **Interesting cask finishes** or expressions

**Goal:** Give them something NEW to talk about at Garage HQ, not remind them of bottles they already have.
