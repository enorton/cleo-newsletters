# Crew Traditions & Rituals

## 🥃 The Infinity Bottles

The crew maintains **two infinity bottles** with tracked stats:

### Bourbon Infinity Bottle
- Running blend of bourbon contributions over time
- Stats tracked: what's in it, how much added, how much poured
- Evolving flavor profile as new bottles are added

### Scotch Infinity Bottle  
- Running blend of scotch contributions over time
- Stats tracked: what's in it, how much added, how much poured
- Likely heavy on Islay/Island influence (crew favorites)

**Newsletter Angle:**
- Reference infinity bottle contributions when featuring new bottles
- "This would be a killer addition to the scotch infinity bottle"
- Seasonal infinity bottle reviews/evolution tracking
- Anniversary milestones (when did it start? how has it evolved?)

## ✈️ Boy's Trip Planning

**Current Focus:** Cape Breton / Glenora Distillery  
**Why:** Closer, easier, in Canada (vs Kentucky Bourbon Trail or Islay)  
**Location:** Cape Breton, Nova Scotia - Canada's only single malt distillery  
**Vibe:** Maritime scenery, Celtic culture, Canadian whisky pilgrimage

**Newsletter Angle (ONE paragraph per week):**
- Cape Breton / Glenora distillery spotlight
- Maritime whisky culture
- Travel tips, scenery, what to expect
- Glenora expressions to try
- Keep aspirational and titillating (not logistics-heavy)
- Plant seeds, don't detail

**Previous Considerations:**
- Kentucky Bourbon Trail (too far, too expensive)
- Islay Whisky Triangle (Scotland - way too far)
- Cape Breton wins: closer, Canadian, unique, achievable
